**Universal Approximation Theorem**
The **universal approximation theorems (UATs)** state that neural networks with a certain structure can, in principle, approximate any continuous function to any desired degree of accuracy. [Wikipedia Universal Approximation Theorem](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Universal_approximation_theorem)
